(One loses happiness in one field, one would make up in antoher): for \(T_1 =T_2\) and 'long enough': \[\int_{T_1} h(t) dt = \int_{T_2} h(t) dt\] Law of domination: Dominanation is a strategy of rewards in the sake of less security.
Law of submission: Submission is a strategy of security in the sake of rewards.
Happiness in relation to dom/sub: The happiness generated by equal degree of dominance or submission is equal.
